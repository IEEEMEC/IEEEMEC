IEEE EMBS MEC Chapter Awarded

With immense pride, our own IEEE EMBS MEC SBC has been declared the Best EMBS Student Chapter of 2018 in Kerala Section

April 23, 2019

With immense pleasure, we share with you the news of IEEE EMBS MEC SBC being selected as the Best EMBS Student Chapter - 2018 amongst the Kerala Section. This is indeed a golden feather in the cap of IEEE MEC SB for having achieved this feat after its maiden appearance! Congratulations to all those who have been burning the candle at both the ends!

May today's success be the beginning of tomorrow's achievements!
