Talks#MEC

IEEE CS MEC SB in collaboration with the MEC Developer Community presents a talk series by students

February 28, 2019

Talks#MEC is a series of talks by the students of Model Engineering College, in which they get to present developer lessons and experiences they had while working in the field. This series was started in 2019 as an attempt at getting MECians to share their thoughts and evangelise the art of being a maker in the Computer Sciences.
