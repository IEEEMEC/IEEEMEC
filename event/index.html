Events

MEC.conf

A first of its kind developer conference organised by the MEC developer community. With myriads of events like overnight Dev Sprint Hackathon, technical workshops and interactive sessions with the other tech enthusiasts made the event engaging and informative! The event had two Workshops conducted in parallel on the topics Building Recommendation Engines and Memory Forensics.

24th May 2019

XtreMEC

The event was organised with the motive of igniting a formal introduction of IEEE Xtreme and a community participation building session for competitive coding under the banner of ‘XtreMEC’, a Practice Community for MECians. It consisted of a short session on the importance of participating in coding competitions and the career advances one can make in the field of technology by knowing the basics of the same.

20th March 2019

Empneo 3.0

Following the legacy of earlier two editions of Empneo, Empneo 3.0 was successful enough in achieving greater heights under the tagline - illuminating possibilities.The event witnessed talks, workshops and an industrial visit, all wrapped in a layer of fun and excitement. EMPNEO 3.0 saw incredible participation, with over a hundred students participating from schools across the state. The theme was “Spark”, and every niche and corner of the event was planned accordingly.

12th July 2019

Alchemy 2.0

As a pre event to the two-day science camp Empneo 3.0, the student branch also organised a technical quiz called Alchemy. The quiz was conducted across two rounds, the Preliminary and the Final. The top 6 teams to qualify the prelims moved on to the Finals, where finally, Gregorian Public School emerged winners, followed by Bhavans Elamakkara and Bhavans Adarsha as runners-up. The quiz focussed on the participants’ scientific understanding and technical Knowledge.

12th July 2019

Science Day Celebrations

In connection with the science day celebrations, Govt. Model Engineering College(MEC) celebrated 6 events over the span of 2 weeks commencing on 13 February 2019 to 28 February 2019. The events consisted of two talks, a powerpoint presentation, a blogging competition, and an idea pitching competition. As the theme for this 2019's science day was ‘Science for the people and the people for science’, we have managed to incorporate the true essence of it by actively involving people from genres of both experienced and enthusiastic ones.

24th May 2019

BITWISE

To scout potentially talented students and get them interested in competitive programming, BITWISE was organised in association with IEEE Computer Society, MACS & EXCEL 2019 focussing the 1st and second year students of MEC. The first two rounds were conducted on an online platform called HackerRank. This event served as a warm-up for IEEEXtreme to be conducted in October.

24th May 2019

Translation 1.0

The IEEE SB of Govt. Model Engineering College associated with Facebook Developer Circle to conduct a workshop TRANSLATION 1.0, which focussed on two big impact and popular projects: ReactJS and Django and worked on localizing the project to Malayalam, which included translating the documentation and other major aspects of these projects.

24th May 2019

School Outreach Program

The event was organised as an initiative by the MEC.conf with the motive of providing school students an exposure to various technical domains. The selection criteria for the same consisted of an aptitude test based on logical reasoning and data interpretation. The winners of which were given a one day hands on workshop about website development. Truly passionate ones were then gifted with a free pass to attend the MEC.conf.

24th May 2019

Women's Day Celebrations

As a part of the women's day celebrations, IEEE MEC SB in association with WIE affinity group invited over two exceptional achievers of the college ( GHC'18 and GHCI'18 scholars) for the #AspireTalk to ignite the spark within the students to come out of their shell and seize the opportunities and share their mantra of playing it right!

24th May 2019

Graphic Design Workshop

To inculcate in students, the very needed skill of designing, a two-day graphic Designing workshop was organised to give shape to one's imaginations and create magic on screen with the help of Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator.

24th May 2019

Talks#MEC

Talks#MEC is a talk series for the students and by the students to share their love for innovation. In an age where peer-to-peer learning turns out to be the best method to effectively reciprocate novel ideas and knowledge amongst the circle, this series is an initiative by the Computer Society of MEC SB to encourage collaborative learning and foster one's interest in technical talks.
